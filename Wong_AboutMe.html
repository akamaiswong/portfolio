My career goal is to become a computer engineer, specifically in the field of website design. I am currently in the Engineering Academy at my school, Dublin High School, after being accepted in 2017. This is an exclusive program that assists students in furthering their knowledge of the different disciplines of engineering, including computer science, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering. It provides the students in the Academy access to several specialized classes, as well as mentors who are currently in the industry, but is only functional during the school year. Therefore, during the summer, I usually take additional classes to take more AP classes during the semester, or do individual projects.

I love to read any type of fiction novel, ranging from fantasy to historical fiction. Currently, I am at level 10, the highest level, meaning that I am certified to teach piano to others. I am also a Girl Scout, and have achieved the Girl Scout Silver Award. I am currently working on completing my Gold Award, the most prestigious and highest ranking award in Girl Scouts.
