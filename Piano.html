I have played the piano for 8 years, and have since earned the Level 10 Certificate of Merit. This is the highest level in this program, and means that I am qualified to teach piano to others.

I have played the violin for 5 years, and was in the Fallon Middle School Orchestra as well as the Dublin High School Orchestra. During this time, I have performed many concerts with the orchestras as well as competing in a local competition.
